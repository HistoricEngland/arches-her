About

What is the Greater London Historic Environment Reocrd (GLHER)?

The GLHER is a comprehensive and dynamic resource for the historic environment of the Capital city. From the earliest human occupation to the present day, GLHER data promotes better understanding of London's rich heritage, and supports development and regeneration through the work of the Greater London Archaeological Advisory Service (GLAAS).

Volunteering with GLHER

We have opportunities for enthusiastic volunteers, of all ages and backgrounds, to help enhance the Greater London Historic Environment Record. Some are looking for work experience or a change of career, others do it just for fun, but all our volunteers play a critical role developing this resource. Past and current projects include:

  • historic parks and gardens
  • archaeological excavation burial sampling strategies
  • London workhouses, and
  • World War One hospital sites
